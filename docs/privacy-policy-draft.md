# Privacy Policy

Effective date: `[Effective Date]`

Flipod ("Flipod", "we", "our", or "us") provides a mobile language-learning app that helps users listen to short audio clips, save vocabulary, practice comprehension, and personalize their learning experience.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, how long we keep it, and the choices available to you.

If you use the Flipod mobile app or related services, you agree that your information will be handled as described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

- the Flipod mobile application;
- our account, authentication, content, and learning services;
- support and operational services we use to run the app.

It does not apply to third-party websites, apps, or services that we do not control, even if we link to them or use them as service providers.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information depending on how you use Flipod.

### 2.1 Account and Sign-In Information

If you sign in or link an account, we may collect:

- your phone number for SMS verification;
- your Sign in with Apple identifier;
- your name and/or email address if provided through Sign in with Apple and stored as your linked identity display value;
- authentication session information needed to keep you signed in and protect your account.

### 2.2 Device and App Identifiers

We generate and store an app-specific device identifier to support guest sessions, account recovery, local-to-account migration, and session continuity across our services.

We may also process app session identifiers and security-related authentication records.

### 2.3 Profile and Preference Information

We collect profile and preference information that you set or generate in the app, such as:

- your learning level;
- your interests;
- your app language or native language preference;
- theme and onboarding state;
- certain in-app settings and learning preferences.

### 2.4 Learning Activity and Saved Content

We collect information related to your use of the learning experience, including:

- bookmarks and saved clips;
- vocabulary you save or mark as known;
- liked clips and related interaction signals;
- practice progress and review history;
- event data related to how you interact with clips and learning features;
- generated practice state and related study progress.

If you use Flipod in guest mode, some of this information may be stored locally on your device first and later merged into your account if you sign in.

### 2.5 AI, Translation, and Speech Inputs

To provide certain features, we may process content you submit or trigger through the app, including:

- words, interests, and proficiency signals used to generate practice content;
- text submitted for text-to-speech playback;
- clip text and explanations submitted for localization or translation;
- words you choose to look up in dictionary or translation features.

### 2.6 On-Device Storage and Cached Data

The app stores certain information on your device, including:

- authentication tokens stored in secure device storage;
- local device and guest-session state;
- cached profile, bookmarks, vocabulary, practice data, likes, and translations;
- cached audio files used to improve playback performance.

### 2.7 Security and Verification Information

We may collect and keep limited records needed to protect the service, such as:

- SMS verification challenge records;
- cooldown, retry, and abuse-prevention signals;
- authentication session validity and revocation records.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information we collect to:

- create and manage your account;
- verify your identity and support sign-in methods;
- save and sync your learning progress across sessions and devices;
- personalize recommendations and practice content;
- provide vocabulary, translation, and text-to-speech features;
- operate, secure, debug, and improve the app;
- prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access;
- comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

We do not use your information for cross-app advertising tracking.

## 4. How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers that help us operate Flipod. Depending on the feature you use, these providers may process information on our behalf or receive request data needed to fulfill a feature.

Current providers used by the app or backend include:

- Apple, for Sign in with Apple authentication;
- Aliyun SMS, for SMS verification delivery;
- Azure OpenAI, for certain content translation and AI-generated practice features;
- ElevenLabs, for text-to-speech generation;
- Google Translate public endpoints, for certain in-app word lookup and translation requests sent from the client;
- dictionaryapi.dev, for word pronunciation and dictionary lookup requests sent from the client.

We may also disclose information:

- if required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request;
- to protect the rights, safety, or security of Flipod, our users, or others;
- in connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, reorganization, sale of assets, or similar transaction.

We do not sell personal information as part of an advertising data marketplace, and we do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising based on the current Flipod implementation.

## 5. International Data Transfers

Flipod and its service providers may process data in countries other than the one where you live. These countries may have different data protection laws.

Where required, we take steps designed to protect personal information when it is transferred across borders.

## 6. Data Retention

We keep personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain your account, support learning features, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

In general:

- account and learning records are kept while your account remains active;
- security and verification records may be retained for a shorter or longer period as needed for abuse prevention, compliance, and operational integrity;
- on-device cached data may remain on your device until it expires, is overwritten, is cleared by the app, or you remove the app.

## 7. Your Choices and Controls

Depending on how you use Flipod, you may be able to:

- access and update profile information in the app;
- log out of your account;
- use guest mode before creating or linking an account;
- delete your account from within the app.

If you request account deletion, we will delete your account and associated personal data from our active records, except where retention is required or permitted by law, needed for security or fraud prevention, or necessary to complete unresolved operational matters.

Because some data may be stored locally on your device, certain cached or local records may remain on the device until you clear app storage, sign out and reset local state, or remove the app.

## 8. Security

We use measures designed to protect personal information, including authentication controls, secure token storage on device, and service-side session management.

No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Flipod is not intended for use by children in jurisdictions where parental consent is required for personal-data processing, unless such consent has been obtained by a parent or guardian.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us in a way that violates applicable law, contact us at `[Contact Email]`.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may provide notice through the app, our website, or other appropriate means.

The "Effective date" at the top of this Privacy Policy indicates when the latest version became effective.
